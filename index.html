About VAGP Silk Sarees

Based in Arni, Tamil Nadu, VAGP Silk Sarees works directly with local weavers to preserve traditional silk craftsmanship. Our handloom Kanchipuram and Arni silk sarees are woven with pure mulberry silk and 24K gold zari.

Each saree is a masterpiece, taking 7-15 days to weave using techniques passed down for generations. We guarantee authenticity with government-certified silk.
